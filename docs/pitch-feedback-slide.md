# Pitch Deck — Feedback Slide

## Slide title

**We ship what users tell us to ship.**

## Body (3 bullets, concise — designed to read in 20 seconds)

- **Feedback (Apr 2026).** Program Manager, Goshen New Beginnings
  (Cherokee County foster agency):

  > "The absolute best plan would be to identify all the different resources
  > in counties to be incorporated as part of this plan."

- **Our response (same week).** Expanded curated resources from 27 → 52
  across five new counties — Fulton, Gwinnett, Clayton, Chatham, and
  Cherokee. Added a statewide 211 Georgia fallback so any of Georgia's 159
  counties has a verified next step, even one we haven't curated yet.

- **Why it matters.** Nest isn't a one-shot hackathon artifact — it has a
  real feedback loop with the people who work with foster youth every day.
  The judges are watching the loop in action.

## Speaker notes (say aloud — ~30 seconds)

"One of the first pieces of external feedback we got came from the Program
Manager at Goshen New Beginnings — a foster agency in Cherokee County, north
of here. They told us the biggest gap was county-level depth: the app had
statewide lines and a couple of counties, but not enough local routing.

We shipped the response the same week. We went from 27 verified resources to
52, added five new counties including Cherokee — Goshen's own county — and
built a 211 Georgia fallback so a young person in any of the other 154
counties still has a next step. No pitch is worth shipping if the product
can't respond to real users in real time. This slide is that proof."
